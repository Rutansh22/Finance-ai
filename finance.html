Common Questions

Everything you need
to make the decision.

How does Embeings AI differ from off-the-shelf fintech software?+
Off-the-shelf platforms are built for the median institution. Embeings AI is architected around your specific operational model, data infrastructure, and regulatory obligations. Every model is trained on your institution's own historical data — not industry averages. This means higher accuracy, lower false positives, and full compliance with your internal audit requirements.
What does the onboarding process look like?+
We begin with a 2–3 week Operational Intelligence Audit — mapping your workflows, data flows, and existing systems. From there we design the architecture, deploy in staged phases alongside your legacy systems, and only cut over once all validation criteria are met. Typical full deployment runs 6–10 weeks.
Do we need our own data science or engineering team?+
No. Embeings AI provides the full technical team — data engineers, ML specialists, and integration architects — for the deployment phase. Post-go-live, day-to-day operation requires no specialist in-house resource. Your team works through the platform interface; our team handles all model maintenance and governance.
How is real-time data handled — latency, reliability?+
Our signal processing infrastructure runs at sub-millisecond latency for market data and risk calculations, with 99.97% uptime guaranteed by SLA. We operate on co-located infrastructure in primary financial data centres, with hot standby failover and full disaster recovery capability.
How do you ensure regulatory compliance across jurisdictions?+
Our compliance module is configured per jurisdiction at deployment — covering MiFID II, EMIR, SFTR, Dodd-Frank, and custom internal rules. Regulatory rule sets are maintained by our team and updated automatically. All decisions and alerts carry an explainable audit trail suitable for direct regulator submission.
How is client financial data secured?+
All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Client data is fully isolated in dedicated tenancy environments — never co-mingled with other institutional data. We hold SOC 2 Type II certification, conduct annual penetration testing, and support private cloud deployment for institutions that require on-premises data residency.
